3D Modelling and Rendering: Autodesk Maya

3D modeling and 3D rendering are different stages of the 3D workflow. 3D modeling is the process of developing a mathematical representation an inanimate or living object. Once the object is displayed as a 3D model, rendering can take place. 3D rendering turns the modeling product into a high-quality image, video or animated model.
